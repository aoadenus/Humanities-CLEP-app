c. 1848–1900

Aligned with socialism and photography. Impressionism
c. 1860–1900
 Broken BrushworkOptical Color MixingEn Plein AirCapturing LightFleeting MomentLoose Form Capture the ephemeral impression of light and atmosphere, not a finished "academic" image. The eye sees patches of color, not outlines. MonetRenoirDegasPissarroSisleyMorisot Water Lilies series (Monet) Impression, Sunrise (Monet — named the movement) Luncheon of the Boating Party (Renoir) The Ballet Class (Degas) Paris, France · Rejected by the Académie des Beaux-Arts

Influenced by Cézanne and African masks. Analytical vs. Synthetic phases. Two methods: dreamlike realism vs. automatic writing/drawing.
